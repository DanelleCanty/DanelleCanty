Fun Facts

- The show was filmed primarily in Los Angeles, not Louisiana, though some exterior shots were filmed on location.
- Anna Paquin won a Golden Globe for Best Actress in a Drama Series for her role as Sookie.
- The theme song "Bad Things" by Jace Everett was chosen by creator Alan Ball specifically for its sultry Southern feel.
- Vampires in True Blood cannot enter a home without an invitation — but that invitation can be rescinded.
- The synthetic blood drink "Tru Blood" was actually marketed as a real novelty drink during the show's run.
- Creator Alan Ball also created Six Feet Under, another acclaimed HBO drama.
